Built for Everyone

One platform. Two dashboards.

Whether you're collecting data or acting on it, MunchiesSNHU has a role-based experience designed for you.

For Staff

Collect, manage, and analyze food waste data from across all campus locations. Identify trends, flag pickups, and generate reports.

  • Log waste entries with sensor readings
  • Bulk upload via CSV template
  • View heatmap and best/worst weeks
  • Export filtered CSV reports
  • Print-ready report pages
Staff Login

For Students

See your campus sustainability impact, follow progress toward the composting goal, and engage with the community leaderboard.

  • View real-time campus waste stats
  • Track personal sustainability impact
  • Set and track a pledge goal
  • Location leaderboard rankings
  • Weekly sustainability tips
Student Login
Platform Features

Everything you need to reduce waste

From data collection to AI-powered insights, the full toolkit is built in.

Live Dashboards

Real-time charts for daily waste vs. compost, diversion rates, and week-over-week deltas across every campus location.

MunchiesAI Assistant

Ask questions in plain English. The AI reads live campus data and responds with grounded, actionable insights — not generic advice.

Bulk CSV Import

Upload entire weeks of waste data at once with drag-and-drop. Row validation and preview before anything hits the database.

Location Heatmap

See which buildings and dining areas generate the most waste — ranked by volume and diversion rate to prioritize resources.

Campus Leaderboard

Locations compete on diversion rate. Students can see rankings and rally their dining hall toward the 50% composting goal.

Reports & Export

Filter by date, location, or status — then export to CSV or print a formatted report for sustainability meetings and audits.
