FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Visit the sign-up page and fill in your name, SNHU email, and choose your role (Staff or Student). Your account is created instantly — no approval wait. Staff accounts get access to the full data entry and reporting suite; student accounts get the campus stats dashboard and leaderboard.
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact us at sustainability@snhu.edu.
MunchiesAI is the AI sustainability assistant built into every dashboard. It has live access to campus waste data and can answer questions like "What is our diversion rate?" or "Which location wastes the most food?" — look for the robot button in the bottom-right corner of any dashboard page after you sign in.
Sign in as a Staff account and navigate to Bulk Import in the navigation. Download the blank template, fill in your data (required columns: foodType, weight, date, location), then drag and drop the file onto the import page. A preview will show which rows are valid before anything is saved.
Yes — MunchiesSNHU is free for all SNHU staff and students. There are no tiers, no paid features, and no limits on data entry or reporting. It's funded as part of SNHU's campus sustainability initiative.
Yes. On the Reports page (Staff accounts), filter by date range, location, or status and click Export CSV to download a spreadsheet — or click Print Report for a formatted, print-ready layout suitable for sustainability meetings and audits.
The diversion rate is calculated as: lbs composted ÷ total lbs logged × 100. Only published entries are included. SNHU's current goal is to reach a 50% diversion rate — you can track progress in real time on the staff and student dashboards.
